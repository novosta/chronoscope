About Chronoscope

Chronoscope Records is an independent electronic pop music label dedicated to supporting emerging artists with professional management, comprehensive music protection, and strategic release planning.

We provide exclusive artist partnerships with fair revenue sharing, monthly release schedules, and industry-leading intellectual property protection to help artists focus on creating while we handle the business.

Our roster focuses on innovative electronic pop artists ready to make their mark on the music industry with consistent, high-quality releases.
